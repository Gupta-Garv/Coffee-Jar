Welcome to Coffee Jar

Your cozy escape in the heart of Pune. Whether you're looking for the perfect cup of coffee or a warm space to unwind, Coffee Jar offers the ideal ambiance for every moment.

We take pride in serving freshly brewed coffee, delectable snacks, and delicious pastries. Our menu is carefully curated to meet the diverse tastes of our loyal patrons, whether you're a coffee connoisseur or someone who just enjoys a good cup of tea.

About Us

Coffee Jar was born out of a passion for great coffee and creating a space where people could come together, relax, and enjoy good conversations. With its roots in Pune, Coffee Jar has become a local favorite, known for its intimate vibe, friendly staff, and, of course, exceptional coffee.

Our café isn't just about coffee; it's about building connections. Whether you're catching up with friends, working on your latest project, or simply indulging in your love for coffee, we aim to offer an experience that feels like home.

Our commitment to quality is reflected in every cup we serve. We source our beans from the best coffee growers, ensuring that each sip is as fresh and flavorful as possible. Come visit us and experience the cozy charm that has made us a staple in the community.
